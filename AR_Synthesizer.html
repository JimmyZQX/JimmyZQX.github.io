Seamless Instrumental Playing in a Virtual Environment

The video on the top is a modular synthesizer that runs in an augmented reality environment. The user plays the physical midi keyboard that is wirelessly connected to the headset and the headset calculates the pitches and the wave signals of the oscillators. My surroundings are not shown due to privacy concerns. The dark area in the whole background should be my surrounding with a midi keyboard that I am playing.

The second video is a surround sound system. The players can place the speakers to different locations to get the sounds to come from different directions. This makes surround sound configuration much simpler to understand than existing methods. I am demonstrating the program in the Unity editor, but it should work the same when running on the headset, and the user can use their hands to move the speakers.
