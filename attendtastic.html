Location-Based Game to Improve School Attendance Rate

In order to boost the engagement of the students when studying at school, we built a mobile app that gave students fun reasons to go to campus by collecting characters that could only be collected on campus. The solution took inspiration from the popular Pokemon Go game, likewise other location-based AR games. Our solution is a response to: "Challenge #1 | Digital education: Think of a solution that leverages real-time engagement data and/or artificial intelligence to help schools in under-resourced communities improve graduation rates, student attendance, higher-ed enrollment, teacher retention, or proficiency on state assessments.
